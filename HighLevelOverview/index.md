# Documentation

Comprehensive documentation for WorldSim's AI and economic systems.

---

## Main Categories

### [AI Systems](AI_Systems)

Personality-driven NPCs creating emergent narratives through:
- 7-trait personality system driving all behavior
- Multi-dimensional relationships
- Dynamic factions and political intrigue
- Emergent occupations and careers
- Memory, learning, and reputation systems

**Browse**: [Core Systems](AI_Systems/Core/01_Executive_Summary) | [Enhancements](AI_Systems/Enhancements/01_Overview)

### [Economic Systems](Economic_Systems)

Observable, physical economy with:
- Real resource extraction and production
- Physical markets and face-to-face trade
- Acquaintance networks for discovery
- Professional requirements driving demand
- Emergent merchant class and trade routes

**Browse**: [Core Systems](Economic_Systems/Core/01_Core_Philosophy) | [Enhancements](Economic_Systems/Enhancements/01_Overview)
